About Me

I currently work as the Curriculum Director at a non-profit called All Star Code. Our mission is to address the lack of diversity in tech by focusing early in the pipeline, young men of color in high school. I've created a mobile web development curriculum that gives our students the exposure to tech that is often lacking in their schools and communities.

Before working for All Star Code, I fell for programming mid-way through my masters, and have been teaching myself how to code ever since.

Thus far, my focus have been on teaching, and I've enjoyed engaging with our future tech leaders, however, over the past few years I've become more interested in focusing on web development.

My passion is to help improve people's lives through technology.
